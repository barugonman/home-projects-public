# October 2026 RV Trip — Seattle → Los Angeles

**Style:** RV/van, full-hookup sites. Fast I-5 spine with one coastal Redwoods detour.
**Window:** First two weeks of October 2026. Plan below departs **Fri, Oct 2** and arrives LA **Wed, Oct 14, 2026** (13 nights).
**Total:** ~1,135 mi / ~18 hrs driving + ~5 hrs for the Redwoods detour.

> ⚠️ **I can't book on your behalf or see live availability on your account.** This is a research plan — the actual reservations happen on Recreation.gov / ReserveCalifornia / Oregon State Parks / each park's own site.

---

## ⏰ Timing alert — book now

Recreation.gov (federal), ReserveCalifornia (CA state parks), and Oregon State Parks all use a **rolling 6-month window**. For Oct 1–14, 2026 arrivals, those windows opened in **early-to-mid April 2026** — they are **open right now** (today is June 25, 2026) and popular coastal/redwoods sites are already filling.

- Lock **state/federal** sites first (highest demand): Champoeg, Valley of the Rogue, Redwoods-area.
- **Private RV resorts** (KOA, Grapevine, Seven Feathers, Sacramento River) book further out / are lower-risk — fine to do second.
- **Weekend nights fill fastest** — flagged below: Portland (Oct 2–3) and Sacramento (Oct 9–10).

## 🏕️ Campground options by region (research detail)

### Washington / Oregon
- **Champoeg State Park** — a couple miles off I-5 south of Portland; very nice.
- **Jantzen Beach RV Park / Rose City RV Park** — Portland, full hookups.
- **Armitage Park Campground** — Eugene area; spacious sites with hookups.
- **Valley of the Rogue State Park** — right off I-5 in Southern Oregon; full hookups.
- **Seven Feathers Resort RV Park** — first-class resort (casino-associated), luxury amenities.
- **Ashland's Creekside Campground & RV Park** — creek access near I-5; RVs up to 60 ft, full hookups.

### Redwoods detour (CA/OR border)
- **Crescent City/Redwoods KOA** — full-hookup, 30/50A, big-rig friendly; near Redwood NSP trails & beaches.
- **Klamath River RV Park** — clean, gated, river views.
- **Chinook RV Resort / Kamp Klamath / Driftwood RV Park** (~$49/nt) — other strong full-hookup options.

### Northern California (I-5)
- **Sacramento River RV Park (Redding)** — full hookup, 100+ ft pull-throughs, 30/50A, pool, river views. (530) 365‑6402.
- **JGW RV Park (Redding)** — 32 acres, oak trees, pool, pet area. (530) 365‑7965.
- **Redding RV Park** — just off I-5 south of Shasta Lake; up to 67 ft, full hookups.
- **Red Bluff KOA Journey** — riverfront back-in + pull-thru, 50/30/20A.

### Central Valley → LA
- **RiverPoint Landing Marina Resort (Stockton)** — minutes off I-5 on the San Joaquin; 30/50A; $37–65; dump station, showers, laundry, WiFi.
- **Grapevine RV Resort (Lebec)** — 187 pull-throughs, full hookups; where I-5 meets Hwy 99, ~15 mi S of Bakersfield; pool, laundry, dog runs.
- **Bakersfield River Run RV Park** — full hookups, cable, WiFi, on the Kern River (off Hwy 99).

## 🌦️ Things to weigh
- **October weather:** Oregon coast / Redwoods can turn wet by mid-October; the I-5 valley and SoCal stay dry and warm. The "mostly fast" plan is the weather-safer bet too.
- **Rig length:** most picks above take 60+ ft, but confirm per-site. (Provide rig length to flag tight ones.)
- **Weekends:** Portland (Oct 2–3) and Sacramento (Oct 9–10) land on Fri/Sat — book those first.
